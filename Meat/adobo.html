Chicken Adobo

Description:

Chicken adobo is a typical Filipino dish that showcases the perfect harmony of flavors. Pieces of chicken are marinated and simmered in a rich blend of soy sauce, sugar, vinegar, calamansi, garlic, bay leaves, and black pepper, The process of marinate and slow-cooking allows the flavor to be absorbed by the chicken, resulting in a rich, tender, and aromatic dish. This recipe is good for 4 to 5 people and is easy to prepare. So, grab thos ingredients, turn up the excitement, and let the adobo magic unfold!

Ingredients:

  • 1kg chicken
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2-3 calamansi
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soysauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 5 pcs bay leave
  • 2 teaspoon sugar

Procedures:

  1. Marinate the chicken with soysauce, garlic, pepper, bay leaves, and calamansi for 1-2 hours.
  2. Saute the onion, garlic, and marinated chicken, for 5-8 mins or until the chicken is medium brown.
  3. Once the chicken is tender, add the water, sugar, pepper, bay leaves, and additonal soysauce. Let it boil for another 5-8 mins before adding vinegar.
  4. After adding vinegar, make sure not to stir. Let simmer for 5 mins and taste.
