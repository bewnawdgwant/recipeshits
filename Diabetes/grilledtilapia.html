Grilled Tilapia

Description:

This not only imparts a savory and delicious taste but also aligns with dietary considerations. Ingredients:

  • 1-2 pieces of tilapia
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Calamansi
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooking oil

Procedures:

  1. Combine the tomatoes, onion, salt, pepper, and calamansi juice in a bowl.
  2. Stuff the mixture into the tilapia.
  3. Brush oil and salt on the outer part of tilapia. You may wrap the fish in aluminum foil and grill it or you may grill it over a pan, just use a cover for air circulation.
  4. Grill the tilapia for 10 minutes per side
  5. Remove the aluminum foil and serve the tilapia with soy sauce, onion, and calamansi as sauce.
