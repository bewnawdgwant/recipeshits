Ingredients:

  • 1kl ground chicken
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon ginger
  • 1 can whole water chestnuts or almond nuts
  • Onion leaves
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lettuce

Procedures:

  1. Add the olive oil to a heated pan. Saute the garlic, onion, ginger, and ground chicken for 8-10 mins.
  2. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, and onion leaves. Occasionally stir for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Mix the water chestnuts or almond nuts, the substitute, and let it cook for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Once cooked, wrap two or three tablespoons of chicken mixture into the lettuce leaf.
