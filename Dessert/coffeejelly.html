Coffee Jelly

Description:

Coffee jelly is a refreshing and delightful dessert that combines the bold flavors of coffee with the silky texture of jelly. It's made by setting a coffee-infused gelatin mixture and cutting it into cubes. Served with sweetened cream, it's a delightful treat for coffee lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 2 packets unflavored gelatin (about 14g)
  • 2 cups brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons condensed milk

Procedures:

  1. Dissolve the gelatin in the brewed coffee and let it sit for a few minutes.
  2. Heat the mixture over low heat, stirring until the gelatin completely dissolves. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  3. Pour the mixture into a square dish and refrigerate until set (about 4 hours or overnight).
