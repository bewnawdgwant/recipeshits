Mango Graham Cake

Description:

Mango Graham Cake is a no-bake Filipino dessert that combines layers of graham crackers, sweetened mangoes, and a luscious cream filling. It's a delightful and easy-to-make treat that's perfect for any occasion, especially during hot summer days.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, sliced
  • 2 packs graham crackers
  • 2 cans (14 oz each) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 packs (8 oz each) all-purpose cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Procedures:

  1. In a bowl, combine the sweetened condensed milk, all-purpose cream, and vanilla extract. Mix until well incorporated.
  2. In a rectangular dish, layer graham crackers at the bottom.
  3. Spread a portion of the cream mixture over the graham crackers, then add a layer of sliced mangoes.
  4. Repeat the layers until you reach the desired height, finishing with a layer of cream on top.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld together.
