Banana Oatmeal Pancakes

Description

If you are looking for something sweet or you want an easy breakfast recipe that is good for your health, banana oatmeal pancake is for you. The use of oats contributes to a high-fiber content, which may help manage blood pressure levels. Additionally, ripe bananas offer natural sweetness without added sugars that can impact cardiovascular health. This breakfast option is low in sodium, and by opting for whole-grain oats, it provides complex carbohydrates that are beneficial for heart health. Topped with fresh fruits, which further boost nutritional value, these pancakes offer a delicious and mindful way to support overall cardiovascular well-being for those with hypertension.

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 Bananas
  • Oatmeals
  • 2 Eggs
  • Â½ teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • Unsalted butter

Procedures:

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl.
  2. Mix the oatmeal, eggs, baking powder, and pinch of salt.
  3. In a pan, add butter and fry one to two tablespoons of the mixture, depending on the size you want to make.
  4. Serve it with maple syrup or any fruit you want.
