Stir-fry Shrimp and Mushrooms

Description:

Treat your taste buds and your heart with stir-fried shrimp and mushrooms! It's a good choice if you're watching your blood pressure. With its low-sodium approach to stir-frying, the dish maintains a delicate balance, enhanced by the aromatic fusion of garlic, ginger, and butter. Plus, it has things called antioxidants that are good for your heart. It's quick, tasty, and a smart pick for your health. Enjoy it over rice or noodles for a yummy and heart-friendly meal!

Ingredients:

  • Â½ kilo shrimp
  • 250-300 g mushroom
  • 5 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Paprika
  • 5 Garlic cloves
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 tablespoon parsley
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon calamansi juice

Procedures:

  1. Peel the shrimp or you can buy peeled shrimp for less hassle.
  2. In a large pan, saute the garlic and onion with olive oil for 1 minute. Add the shrimp and wait for it to be golden brown.
  3. After 5-7 minutes, add the mushroom, parsley, butter, and calamansi juice.
  4. After a minute, add the paprika, salt, and pepper to taste. Before turning off the heat, kindly check if the chicken is tender enough.
  5. Once all ingredients are cooked, serve and enjoy with pasta or rice.
