Chicken and Asparagus

Description:

This chicken and asparagus dish offers a heart-healthy choice for individuals with hypertension. Lean chicken provides essential protein without excess saturated fat, while asparagus contributes valuable nutrients like potassium and fiber. By opting for low-sodium seasoning alternatives and focusing on fresh ingredients, this flavorful dish supports blood pressure management. Its nutritional profile, rich in potassium and low in saturated fats, aligns with dietary recommendations for those seeking to maintain cardiovascular health.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt and ground pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Soy sauce
  • Chicken broth or Chicken cube
  • Corn starch
  • Ginger
  • Green or chili peppers
  • Olive oil
  • Asparagus
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Procedures:

  1. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. For the sauce, combine the chicken broth or cube that is diluted in water, corn starch, soy sauce, and half cup of water.
  3. Saute the garlic, onion, and ginger. After 1-2 minutes, add the chicken and wait for it to be slightly cooked.
  4. After 8-10 minutes, add the asparagus and the sauce mixture. Let it simmer over medium heat until vegetables and chicken are cooked.

Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories: 250 kcal
  • Fat: 4 g
  • Sodium: 160 mg
  • Protein: 40 g
  • Carbohydrates: 18 g
