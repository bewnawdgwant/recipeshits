Munggo Guisado

Description:

Munggo guisado is a hearty and nutritious dish that begins by sautéing garlic, onions, and tomatoes until they release their flavorful essence. Then, the munggo beans are added and simmered to a delightful tenderness, absorbing the rich flavors of the sautéed ingredients. This dish often includes a medley of vegetables such as spinach, malunggay, or ampalaya, enhancing both the taste and nutritional content. So, tie on your apron, gather your ingredients, and let the joy of cooking lead you to a delightful experience that nourishes both body and soul.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup munggo beans
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 pack chicharon
  • Spinach or Malunggay
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • Salt

Procedures:

  1. Soak the munggo beans in water for 2-3 hours before cooking. But, you can also directly start cooking without soaking the beans.
  2. In a pan, add the cooking oil. Saute the onion, tomato, and garlic.
  3. Add the munggo beans and water. Over medium heat, bring it to a boil.
  4. Add the chicharon, fish sauce, and salt to taste.
  5. Let it cook for another 5-10 minutes or until the munggo beans are softened.
  6. Turn off the heat and enjoy the healthy munggo guisado!
